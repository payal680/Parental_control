About Parental Control Dashboard

Our Parental Control Dashboard is designed to help parents monitor and manage their children's digital activities effectively. With increasing screen time and multiple apps on devices, it becomes essential to ensure a balanced and safe digital environment for kids.

This platform provides real-time insights into app usage, screen time, and digital behavior. Parents can assign tasks, set daily app limits, and get notifications if children exceed allowed usage. The system ensures responsible technology use while allowing freedom for educational content.

The dashboard includes visual charts and reports, showing weekly and daily usage trends. This helps parents make informed decisions and guide children without being intrusive, fostering healthy digital habits.

Our mission is to provide families with an easy-to-use tool to supervise screen time, manage app usage, and promote productivity and learning. By combining clear data visualization with customizable controls, our platform makes digital parenting efficient and stress-free.

We continuously update the system to add new features, improve monitoring accuracy, and make it easier for parents to engage with their child's digital life safely.
